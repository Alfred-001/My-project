Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Home | Products | Cart | About | Contact | Terms

1. How do I place an order?

You can browse our products on the Products page and click "Add to Cart" for items you want to purchase. Then go to the Cart to review and place your order.

2. What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to major cities and towns. You can check delivery availability at checkout by entering your pin code.

3. What are the delivery charges?

Delivery is free for orders above ₹499. A small fee of ₹30 is applied for smaller orders.

4. What is your return policy?

If any item is damaged or incorrect, please contact us within 24 hours for a replacement or refund.

5. Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 1 PM, subject to product availability and delivery area.

6. How can I contact customer support?

You can use the Contact page to send us a message, or email us at support@greenbasket.com.

7. Are your products organic?

We offer a range of organic products. Each product listing specifies whether it is organic or conventional.
